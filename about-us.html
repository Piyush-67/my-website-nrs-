Meet

Our Founder

Mr. Ravinandan Prasad Verma

Director

Introducing a stellar performer and one of the youngest and energetic entrepreneurs Mr. Ravinandan Prasad Verma, who possesses immense knowledge relating to the industry, he belongs. After completing his education from Ranchi, Jharkhand, he decided to move to Kolkata.

With this aspiration, he started as a normal employee in a small organization where his mission to go one step further proved to be inclusive of his aspirations. He ventured into small business opportunities and created the opening of his vision. Slowly in the year 2014 he formed NRS Business Centre Pvt. Ltd. The beginning of NRS Group paved the way for his vision. Since then, he has never looked back and today he is the rightful owner of NRS Group.

 

Our Mission

To act collectively as a group so that all the business wings can have maximum benefits from our passionate effort. The target is always to provide state-of-the-art services to the clients where quality is the first priority.

Our Vision

To be accredited as one of the esteemed organizations whose hunger for excellence is proven by the quality of their service and whose thirst for betterment keeps it a step ahead from the rest of its competitors.

Brand Philosophy

Quality services demand quality people. With this mindset, we spearhead our brands to those who strive for quality and strengthen us to deliver world-class services. We aspire to take each and every feedback to our hearts and work towards performing better.
